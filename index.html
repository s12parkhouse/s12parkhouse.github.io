Top 10 Most Popular Dog Breeds

Read Time: 5 minutes

Around 29% of UK adults own a dog according to pet charity PDSA, which accounts for a staggering 11 million dogs. But what are the most popular breeds? We’ve pulled together the top 10 based on data from the Kennel Club, and added some of our own favourites to boot. But, while all of these dogs are widely adored, some come with particular breed-related health issues. So, after pairing with the right dog for you, the most important next step is to get appropriate pet insurance in place.
